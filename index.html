HISTORY


Amelia and Uzma created Animation Smackdown in 2017. They were two friends that enjoyed art and thought there
should be a competition where people can share and compare artwork. The first competition started small, however the news spread and more artists started to join. This led to today's competition, that we all know.

WINNERS




2019


2D Winner:



6th grade animator BartholomewJr was challenged to animate Book Characters, any character in a novel can be animated.
BartholomewJr creatively expressed their love for their novel and animation.

Book Characters Requirements:

Novel-
Warrior Cats

Character-
Firestar, Tigerstar, Leopardstar

What are they doing?
Cat politics

3D Winner:



12th grade animator CaptainClock was challenged to animate Medival, if it coes from the mediaval ages, animate it.

Medival Requirements:

Item-
Ocean Landscape

Place-
Waves
